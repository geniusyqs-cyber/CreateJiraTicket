Affected Product: MarketSmith Hong Kong (Ticket Ref: #16755)

User Story Name: As data engineer & product analyst, I need to resolve two core HK financial data issues: missing YoY profit growth rate for A+H dual-listed secondary new stocks, and inconsistent financial/rating/industry data for stocks with different share classes of the same underlying company, to restore accurate stock screening & TOP33 ranking logic for end users.

Deliverable:
Complete quarterly YoY earnings growth rate calculation logic for A+H dual-listed secondary new stocks;
Unified financial metrics, stock rating and industry classification for multi-share-class stocks of identical underlying firms;
User-facing TOP33 list and financial screening filters can correctly include & evaluate these dual-listed HK stocks.

1. Context and Background
User submitted feedback (#16755) regarding MarketSmith Hong Kong’s incomplete financial data for A+H dual-listed secondary new stocks:
Many companies listed on ChiNext / STAR Market in mainland China conduct secondary fundraising in Hong Kong with strong market performance. These stocks have full quarterly financial filings on the A-share side, but their corresponding HK tickers lack quarterly YoY profit growth metrics.
Without growth rate values:
EPS ratings incorrectly downgrade to grade D;
Users cannot filter stocks by year-over-year earnings growth;
Qualified stocks fail to be included in the HK TOP33 ranking list.
User suggests reusing matching A-share financial data to fill HK-side gaps, as they represent identical underlying businesses.
Internal verification found two root problems:
Data source separation: A-share EPS data comes from cninfo, HK EPS data from Juyuan. Adjusted financial report release cycles between the two markets break YoY growth calculation for certain quarters, leaving growth rate fields blank.
Multi-share class inconsistency: For the same enterprise (e.g. Alibaba-W 09988, Alibaba-WR 89988), financial figures, stock rating scores and industry classification records are mismatched across different HK share classes.

2. Detail Requirement
Resolve missing quarterly YoY profit growth rate for A+H dual-listed secondary new stocks:
Build cross-market data alignment logic to pull complete A-share quarterly financial data for matching HK dual-listed tickers;
Recompute accurate quarterly YoY earnings growth for HK side when native HK source data cannot support calculation;
Ensure growth rate values persist in HK financial tables for screening and rating calculation.
Unify data of multi-share-class HK stocks belonging to the same underlying company:
Standardize core financial metrics (EPS, profit, revenue etc.) for identical firms with different HK stock codes (W / WR share structures);
Synchronize stock rating results and industry classification labels across all related share class tickers of one enterprise;
Validate downstream product logic after data repair:
Confirm EPS rating no longer incorrectly outputs D grade due to missing growth data;
Verify users can successfully filter dual-listed secondary new stocks by earnings YoY growth filters;
Ensure eligible dual-listed stocks are properly captured and displayed within the HK TOP33 ranking list;
Document cross-market financial data synchronization rules for long-term maintenance.

3. Actual Expectation
All A+H dual-listed secondary new HK stocks have complete quarterly YoY profit growth rate data available; EPS rating results become reasonable and consistent with business fundamentals.
Stocks with different HK share classes (W/WR) under the same company carry identical financial metrics, ratings and industry tags.
End users can filter target dual-listed stocks via earnings growth conditions, and qualified candidates will appear normally in HK TOP33 list.
Cross-market financial data complement mechanism is stable to prevent recurring blank growth rate issues caused by mismatched report release cycles.
