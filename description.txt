User Story Name: As Product Manager, I need to analyze collected demands, formulate product planning, arrange development schedules and complete formal demand sign-off process.

Deliverable: Complete demand analysis document, product iteration roadmap, detailed development schedule, fully signed-off demand confirmation form.

1. Context and Background
After collecting customer and internal business demands, the product manager shall take charge of full demand management workflow. It includes analyzing demand rationality and value, designing product solution, arranging development timeline, and getting formal sign-off from relevant stakeholders before development kicks off.

2. Detail Requirement
Analyze all collected demands, verify demand value, scope, feasibility and business priority.
Based on finalized demands, draw up product iteration planning and function roadmap.
Cooperate with development team to split work items and confirm detailed release schedule.
Organize cross-team review, complete demand confirmation and obtain formal stakeholder sign-off.

3. Actual Expectation
All demands are fully analyzed and validated; clear product planning and delivery schedule are released; all stakeholders complete sign-off to confirm the final demand scope before development execution.
